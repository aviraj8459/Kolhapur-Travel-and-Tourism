Popular Temples

Mahalakshmi Temple

The Mahalaxmi Temple in Kolhapur is a sacred 7th-century shrine dedicated to Goddess Mahalaxmi, known for its stunning architecture and spiritual significance. A key pilgrimage site, it draws thousands of visitors, especially during vibrant festivals like Navratri.

Jyotiba Temple

Jyotiba Temple, located atop the Sahyadri Hills near Kolhapur, is a revered shrine dedicated to Lord Jyotiba, an incarnation of Lord Shiva. Famous for its Chaitra Poornima fair and pink gulal celebrations, it offers both spiritual and scenic experiences.

Kopeshwar Temple

The Kopeshwar Temple in Khidrapur, near Kolhapur, is a 12th-century architectural marvel dedicated to Lord Shiva, renowned for its intricate carvings and the unique presence of both Shiva and Vishnu deities within the same sanctum. Situated on the banks of the Krishna River.

Eco-tourism Spots

Kalamwadi Damm

Kalamwadi Dam is a serene water reservoir surrounded by lush greenery, ideal for nature lovers and peaceful picnics. It's a lesser-known yet scenic spot perfect for short day trips near Kolhapur.

Pahnala Fort

Panhala is a historic hill fort near Kolhapur, offering panoramic views and rich Maratha heritage, closely linked to Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. Its cool climate.

Ranakala Lake

Rankala Lake is a picturesque and popular tourist spot in Kolhapur, known for its sunset views, peaceful ambiance, and lakeside promenade. Boating and local food stalls make it a favorite for evening leisure.
